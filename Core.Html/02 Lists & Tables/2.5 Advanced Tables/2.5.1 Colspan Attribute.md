|**[[2.5.1.4 Visuals]]**|Appearance|Wide cell|The cell stretches horizontally; the content centers by default (if `th`).|
|**[[2.5.1.5 Max Value]]**|Limit|1000|Technically 1000, but practically just span what you need.|
