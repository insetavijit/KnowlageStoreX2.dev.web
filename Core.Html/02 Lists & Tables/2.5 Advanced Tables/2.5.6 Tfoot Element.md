|**Subtopic**|**Focus & Purpose**|**Key Concepts / Details**|**One-Line Recall**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**2.5.6 Tfoot Element**|Introduce footer grouping|Summaries, Totals, Footnotes|Groups the summary rows at the bottom of the table.|
|**[[2.5.6.1 Semantic Meaning]]**|Structure|Table calculations|Tells the browser "This result was calculated from the data above".|
|**[[2.5.6.2 Placement]]**|HTML Order|Before or After|In HTML5, `<tfoot>` goes after `<tbody>`. In HTML4, it went *before* `<tbody>` so it could render while data downloaded (legacy quirk).|
|**[[2.5.6.3 Printing]]**|Behavior|Repetition|Just like `<thead>`, the footer can repeat at the bottom of every print page.|
|**[[2.5.6.4 Content]]**|Data|Sum/Average|Usually contains "Total: $500" or data sources.|
|**[[2.5.6.5 Styling]]**|Visuals|Anchoring|Often stuck to the bottom of the scrolling view.|
|**[[2.5.6.6 Accessibility]]**|Navigation|Summary|Screen readers allow users to jump straight to the footer to get the final numbers.|
