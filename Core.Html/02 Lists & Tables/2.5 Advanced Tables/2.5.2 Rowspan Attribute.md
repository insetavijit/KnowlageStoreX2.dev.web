|**2.5.2 Rowspan Attribute**|Explain vertical spanning|Merging rows, Categorization|Merging cells downwards to cover multiple rows.|
|**[[2.5.2.1 Syntax]]**|Attribute|`rowspan="3"`|Tells the cell to take up the space of 3 standard rows.|
|**[[2.5.2.2 Side Labels]]**|Use Case|Categories|Use it to put a "Morning" label next to the 9am, 10am, 11am rows.|
|**[[2.5.2.3 Grid Impact]]**|Complexity|Displacement|Cells in *subsequent* rows must be omitted because the space is already taken.|
|**[[2.5.2.4 Visualization]]**|Mental Model|Tetris|It blocks out space in future rows; you have to "skip" that slot when writing the next `<tr>`.|
|**[[2.5.2.5 Browser Handling]]**|Rendering|Pushing|If you mess up the math, the browser pushes extra cells out to the right, breaking the table.|
|**[[2.5.2.6 Limitation]]**|Pages|Page breaks|Rowspan plays poorly with printing if the span crosses a page break.|
