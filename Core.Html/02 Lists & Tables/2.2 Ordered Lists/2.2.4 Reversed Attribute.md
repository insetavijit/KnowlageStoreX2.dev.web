|**2.2.4 Reversed Attribute**|Explain countdowns|Top 10, Descending order|Count backwards from the total (5, 4, 3, 2, 1).|
|**[[2.2.4.1 Syntax]]**|Boolean|`<ol reversed>`|Just adding the word `reversed` triggers the behavior.|
|**[[2.2.4.2 Calculation]]**|Auto-counting|Total items|The browser counts the items and starts from the total.|
|**[[2.2.4.3 Combining with Start]]**|Overrides|`reversed start="100"`|Starts at 100 and counts down: 100, 99, 98...|
|**[[2.2.4.4 Use Cases]]**|Rankings|Top 10 Charts|Perfect for "Top 10" lists where #1 is at the bottom (or top).|
|**[[2.2.4.5 Accessibility]]**|Announcement|Reversed order|Some screen readers announce that the list is reversed.|
|**[[2.2.4.6 Browser Support]]**|Modern|Ubiquitous|Widely supported in all modern browsers.|
