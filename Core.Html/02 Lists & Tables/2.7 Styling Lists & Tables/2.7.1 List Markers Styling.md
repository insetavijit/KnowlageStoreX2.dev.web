|**Subtopic**|**Focus & Purpose**|**Key Concepts / Details**|**One-Line Recall**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**2.7.1 List Markers Styling**|Visual customization|`list-style-type`, `list-style-image`, `list-style-position`|Changing the dots and numbers into something prettier.|
|**[[2.7.1.1 Type]]**|Shape/Char|`list-style-type: square`|Change the default disc to a circle, square, or none.|
|**[[2.7.1.2 Image]]**|Graphic|`list-style-image: url(...)`|Replace the bullet with a custom icon (though often buggy/hard to align).|
|**[[2.7.1.3 Position]]**|Flow|`inside` vs `outside`|Decides if the bullet sits *in* the text block or hangs to the left of it.|
|**[[2.7.1.4 Color]]**|Pseudo-element|`::marker`|Use `li::marker { color: red; }` to change the bullet color independently of the text.|
|**[[2.7.1.5 Removal]]**|Reset|`list-style: none`|The most common style: removing bullets entirely for navigation menus.|
|**[[2.7.1.6 Alignment]]**|Refinement|Pseudo-elements|For perfect custom icons, devs often disable default styles and use `::before`.|
