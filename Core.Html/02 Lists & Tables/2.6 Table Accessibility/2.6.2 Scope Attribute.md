|**Subtopic**|**Focus & Purpose**|**Key Concepts / Details**|**One-Line Recall**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**2.6.2 Scope Attribute**|Detail header direction|`col`, `row`, `colgroup`, `rowgroup`|Explicitly tells the browser which direction a header applies.|
|**[[2.6.2.1 scope="col"]]**|Vertical|Down|This header applies to all cells in the column below it.|
|**[[2.6.2.2 scope="row"]]**|Horizontal|Right|This header applies to all cells in the row to the right of it.|
|**[[2.6.2.3 Ambiguity]]**|Problem|Corner cells|Without scope, browsers guess; scope removes the guessing.|
|**[[2.6.2.4 Groups]]**|Spanning|`colgroup`|This header applies to a group of columns (e.g., "Q1" over Jan/Feb/Mar).|
|**[[2.6.2.5 Best Practice]]**|Rule|Always use|Add scope to *every* `<th>` element, even simple ones.|
|**[[2.6.2.6 Support]]**|Tech|Robust|Widely supported and the preferred method for most tables.|
