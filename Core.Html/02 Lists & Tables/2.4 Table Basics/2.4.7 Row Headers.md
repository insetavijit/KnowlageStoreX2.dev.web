|**2.4.7 Row Headers**|Cover vertical headers|First column context, Scope row|Row headers sit on the side and describe the data to their right.|

|**[[2.4.7.6 Sticky Stickiness]]**|CSS|`left: 0`|Using sticky positioning to keep row labels visible on wide tables.|
