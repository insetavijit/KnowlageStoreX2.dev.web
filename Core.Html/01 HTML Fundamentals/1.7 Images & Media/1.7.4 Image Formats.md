|**1.7.4 Image Formats**|Compare common formats|JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, SVG, AVIF|Choose the right format for the job to balance quality and speed.|
|**[[1.7.4.1 JPEG (.jpg)]]**|Photos|Lossy compression, no transparency|Best for photographs with many colors.|
|**[[1.7.4.2 PNG (.png)]]**|Graphics|Lossless, Transparency|Best for logos, screenshots, and images with transparent backgrounds.|
|**[[1.7.4.3 WebP (.webp)]]**|Modern Standard|Google's format|Superior compression offering both lossy and lossless features.|
|**[[1.7.4.4 SVG (.svg)]]**|Vectors|Scalable math|Best for icons and logos; infinite scaling without pixelation.|
|**[[1.7.4.5 GIF (.gif)]]**|Animations|Legacy, Limited colors|Used for simple animations (though video is often better).|
|**[[1.7.4.6 AVIF (.avif)]]**|The Future|Next-gen compression|Better than WebP but check browser support.|
