|**1.6.6 Telephone Links (tel)**|Explain click-to-call|`tel:` URI, Mobile behavior, International format|Turns a phone number into a clickable dialer link.|
|**[[1.6.6.1 Basic Syntax]]**|The protocol|`tel:5551234567`|Use `tel:` followed by the number.|
|**[[1.6.6.2 Formatting]]**|E.164 Standard|`+1-555-000-0000`|Always use International format (plus sign, country code) for safety.|
|**[[1.6.6.3 Mobile vs Desktop]]**|Behavior|Dialer app vs Skype/Facetime|On phones it calls; on desktop it opens a VoIP app.|
|**[[1.6.6.4 Visuals]]**|Readability|Display vs Href|Href is for the machine (`+1555`); Link text is for humans (`(555) ...`).|
|**[[1.6.6.5 SMS Links]]**|Alternative|`sms:` protocol|Use `sms:` to open the messaging app instead of the dialer.|
|**[[1.6.6.6 Auto-Detection]]**|Meta tags|Disabling auto-link|Mobile browsers try to guess phone numbers; control this with meta tags.|
