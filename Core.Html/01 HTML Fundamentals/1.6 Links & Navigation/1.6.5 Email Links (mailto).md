|**Subtopic**|**Focus & Purpose**|**Key Concepts / Details**|**One-Line Recall**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**1.6.5 Email Links (mailto)**|Cover email protocols|`mailto:` URI scheme, Subject/Body params|Opens the user's default email client with a new draft.|
|**[[1.6.5.1 Basic Syntax]]**|Simple link|`mailto:user@example.com`|Clicking opens Outlook/Mail app addressed to the user.|
|**[[1.6.5.2 Parameters]]**|Pre-filling fields|`?subject=Hi&body=Details`|You can prepopulate the subject line and body text.|
|**[[1.6.5.3 Multiple Recipients]]**|CC and BCC|`?cc=manager@example.com`|You can copy other people on the email automatically.|
|**[[1.6.5.4 URL Encoding]]**|Spaces/Special chars|`%20` for space|You must URL encode the values for subject and body.|
|**[[1.6.5.5 UX Warning]]**|Client dependency|No client installed?|It fails if the user doesn't have an email app installed.|
|**[[1.6.5.6 Spam Risk]]**|Scraping|Bot harvesting|Spam bots harvest email addresses from mailto links easily.|
