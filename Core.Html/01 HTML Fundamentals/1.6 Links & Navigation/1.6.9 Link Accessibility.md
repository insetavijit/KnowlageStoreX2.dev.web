|**1.6.9 Link Accessibility**|Cover accessible link practices|Descriptive text, ARIA, Focus states|Links must make sense when read out of context.|

|**[[1.6.9.3 Title Attribute]]**|Tooltip caveat|Redundancy issues|Don't rely on `title` attributes; they aren't accessible to mobile/keyboard users.|
|**[[1.6.9.4 Focus Indicators]]**|Visuals|Outline removal|Never remove the CSS `outline` on focus unless you replace it.|
