|**1.5.6 Deleted and Inserted Text**|Cover document edits|`<del>`, `<ins>`, Tracking changes|Use `<del>` for removed content and `<ins>` for added content.|
|**[[1.5.6.1 Strikethrough Semantics]]**|Meaning of deletion|Removed/corrected information|`<del>` says "this was here, but it's wrong/old now".|
|**[[1.5.6.2 Insertion Semantics]]**|Meaning of addition|New/updated information|`<ins>` says "this is new content added to the document".|
|**[[1.5.6.3 Visual Defaults]]**|Browser styling|Strikethrough vs Underline|Browsers cross out deletions and underline insertions.|
|**[[1.5.6.4 Attributes]]**|Metadata|`cite`, `datetime`|You can explain *why* and *when* the change happened.|
|**[[1.5.6.5 Use Cases]]**|Examples|To-do lists, diff views, price drops|Original Price: ~~$50~~ $25.|
